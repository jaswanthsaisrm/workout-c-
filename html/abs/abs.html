BEGINNER WORKOUT
this is day 1

Russian Twists

x32

Sit, knees bent. Twist torso, touch ground each side. Core engaged.


Push-Ups

x20

Start in a plank position, lower your body until your chest nearly touches the ground, then push back up.


Burpees

x15

Begin in a standing position, drop into a squat, then kick your feet back to a plank position. Do a push-up, then jump back to the squat position and jump up with arms overhead.


Jumping Jacks

x20

Start with feet together and arms at your sides, then jump while simultaneously spreading your legs and raising your arms above your head.


Plank

Hold for 1 minute

Get into a push-up position but with your weight on your forearms instead of your hands, and hold the position, keeping your body straight from head to heels.


Squats

x20

Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, bend your knees and lower your body until your thighs are parallel to the ground, then return to standing position.


Jump Lunges

x15 per leg

Start in a lunge position, then jump up, switching legs mid-air so you land in a lunge position with the opposite leg forward.
